Designer

The designer needs to have a high degree of expertise in design principles and how to best apply them when doing the project. Like the others, they need to know the limits and properly convey this to the rest of the team with communication skills and teamwork. They need to have innovative thinking skills, so the design is easily understood yet complex enough to not be bare bones.

Skills and Experience

  • Communication skills
  • Keen eye on detail
  • Team coordinator supervisor experience
