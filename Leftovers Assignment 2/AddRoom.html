Building Tools

Darien is in the process of renovating his room and is looking for furniture to furnish his room. He is planning to change his flooring and the colour of his walls but is not able to envision the finish product in his head. Darien downloads FlippingRooms which allows him to remodel his room on his phone. On the app, Darien is able to pick different types of furniture,materials and add partitions to his desired new room.

    1. Darien clicks on FlippingRooms app
    2. Darien logs in / signs up on the app
    3. Darien clicks on the 'Start remodelling' button
    4. Darien clicks on the 'Add a room' button on the features page
    5. Darien types in what floor his room is on
    6. Darien choose a room type from the 'Residential' or 'Commercial' button
    7. Darien clicks on 'Start project' button
    8. Darien clicks on 'New project' button
    9. Darien is able to name the project,input the estimation for the project and save the project
    10. Darien changes the name of the floor
    11. Darien picks from the tabs 'Furniture', 'Material' and 'Partition' to remodel
